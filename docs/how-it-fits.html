7shifts Marketing OS

How it fits together

You're getting two things this week. They stack, so you don't pick one.

Yours

Your Workspace

Your own Claude Code setup: folders, your CLAUDE.md, your memory, and the habits that keep it usable past month two. This is where you do your work, and it never leaves your machine.

Shared

The Marketing OS

The shared brain everyone works from: brand guidelines, ICP and segment sizing, positioning, the seven personas, voice rules, and our skills. One repo, one copy, everyone pulls from it.

Why two and not one

If we each keep our own copy of the brand guidelines, the day brand changes the palette, most of them are quietly wrong. The shared brain means we update once and everyone's next piece of work is current. It also gets smarter every time someone feeds a learning back in, which a pile of private folders never does.

It runs both ways

You pull from the brain constantly: copy, campaigns, the ICP, a persona, a skill. You push back to it when you learn something worth keeping, and the share-learning skill does the git for you, so you never touch a command line.

Your workspace is your own station. The Marketing OS is the walk-in the whole kitchen shares. You don't each buy your own tomatoes, and when you figure out a better prep, you put it back in the walk-in for everyone.

To get going

  1. Set up your own Claude Code workspace.
  2. Clone the Marketing OS next to it:
    git clone https://github.com/justinholmes-7shifts/7shifts-marketing-os.git
  3. Point your CLAUDE.md at the brain so it loads every session.

Giving back

When a campaign teaches you something durable, tell Claude "share this learning." It checks the lesson is worth keeping, writes it into the brain, and pushes it so the whole team gets it on their next pull. Keep your drafts and work-in-progress in your own workspace. The brain holds the shared, finished, reusable stuff.

What's in the brain

  • context/ — company context, positioning, ICP and sizing, personas, voice, brand guidelines and assets, framework
  • skills/ — brand-designer, lifecycle-campaign-strategy, clear-communications-v2, share-learning
  • campaigns/ — the shared campaign record
  • learnings.md — the team's accumulated lessons

Your workspace, the shared brain. Set yours up once, pull from the same source, give back as you learn.
