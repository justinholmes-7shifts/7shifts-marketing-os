# How the two Claude Code things fit together

You're getting two things from us this week. They stack, so you don't pick one.

**Tamara's setup doc** stands up your own workspace: folders, your CLAUDE.md, your memory, and the habits that keep it usable past month two. That part is yours.

**The Marketing OS** is the shared brain you clone into it: brand guidelines, ICP and segment sizing, positioning, the seven personas, voice rules, and our skills. One repo, everyone works from the same copy.

Why two and not one: if we each keep our own copy of the brand guidelines, the day brand changes the palette, most of them are quietly wrong. The shared brain means we update once and everyone's next piece of work is current. It also gets smarter every time someone feeds a learning back in, which a pile of private folders never does.

The restaurant version: Tamara's doc sets up your station. The Marketing OS is the walk-in the whole kitchen shares. You don't each buy your own tomatoes.

To get going:

1. Run Tamara's doc to set up your workspace.
2. Clone the Marketing OS into it: `git clone https://github.com/justinholmes-7shifts/7shifts-marketing-os.git`
3. Point your CLAUDE.md at the brain so it loads every session.

Personal workspace, shared brain. Set yours up once, pull from the same source, give back as you learn.
