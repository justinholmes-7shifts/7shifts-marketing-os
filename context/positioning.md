# Positioning

> How we talk about 7shifts. The shared frame every piece of marketing pulls from so we sound like one company. Source: the 2025 Product Messaging Guide ([`source/`](source/)). When positioning changes, this file changes first, then it cascades to voice, campaigns, and readouts.

## Value proposition (customer-facing)

Scheduling, payroll, and tip management in one easy-to-use app. 7shifts helps small and medium-sized restaurants save time, cut costs, and makes their life easier so they can focus on serving their staff and customers.

## Positioning statement (internal only)

For QSR, FSR, and franchised restaurants with 16-75 employees per location who need to effectively manage their teams and labor costs, 7shifts is a restaurant scheduling, payroll, and tip management app that saves time, reduces errors and costs, and gives restaurants peace of mind. Unlike Excel, Toast, Square, Homebase, or HotSchedules, 7shifts is the only tool that is restaurant-specific, easy to use, and built in one app.

> Note on "team management": we moved away from it. It isn't common language among restaurant operators. We lead with the three core products instead (scheduling, payroll, tip management) and call out who we serve best.

## Market category

Workforce management for restaurants. Externally: scheduling, payroll, and tip management for restaurants.

## Competitive landscape

If we didn't exist, here's what a customer would do:
- **All-in-one bundles:** Sling/Toast, Restaurant365, Homebase, Teamwork by SpotOn, Square
- **Point solutions:** When I Work, Deputy, HotSchedules, Jolt, Asana, Harri, Indeed, Toast Payroll, ADP, Gusto, TipHaus, Gratshare, Kickfin, plus SMS/email/Messenger/WhatsApp
- **Manual:** Excel, pen and paper, cash, third-party managed payroll/accounting

Most competitors position around one category (restaurant management software, workforce management) or name 2-3 core products. **The real threat is POS bundling** (Toast, Square, Lightspeed) offering "good enough" scheduling for free, see [`company-context.md`](company-context.md).

People remember three things, not fifteen. Everything ladders up to:

1. **Time savings**
2. **Cost savings**
3. **Increased employee retention**

## Core value props

Pain + benefit, framed as loss-avoidance plus gain. Use these as the backbone of campaign and web copy:

- **One connected app for all your restaurant needs** — using multiple disconnected systems? Consolidate your tech stack and cut costs by moving to one app, and connect your POS data to unlock more.
- **Automate manual processes** — stop spending dozens of hours a month building schedules, calculating tips, and running payroll. Automation gives time back and improves accuracy.
- **Gain back valuable time** — reduce time and errors compiling data from multiple apps. Connect scheduling, time clocking, payroll, and tips so the data flows together.
- **Cut expensive labor costs** — stop paying unbudgeted labor and avoidable compliance fees. Forecast accurate sales and labor to save thousands a year.
- **Make your life easier** — stop your team missing shift updates. Build and change schedules on the go; staff view changes, request swaps, and submit time off.
- **Reduce employee turnover** — one quit can cost thousands to backfill. Keep a pulse on engagement to cut turnover by up to 13%.

## Per-product positioning (one-liners)

- **Scheduling:** easy and built exclusively for restaurants. Managers build and adjust schedules on the go; staff set availability and swap shifts in a free mobile app. *Vs Homebase, Deputy, Sling: easy to use, integrates with your stack, restaurant-specific.*
- **Time Clocking (7punches):** the source of truth for labor data. Track attendance, stay compliant, sync to payroll. Cuts time theft and unbudgeted labor.
- **Payroll:** onboarding, time clocking, compliance, and payroll without leaving the app. (Handle claims carefully, see the payroll sensitivity note in [`company-context.md`](company-context.md).)
- **Tip Management:** end-to-end from collection to payout. Syncs shift data for accurate, automated tip calculations and direct-to-bank payouts.
- **Team Engagement:** pulse surveys, engagement scoring, and feedback to cut the turnover that costs thousands per quit.

## ⚠️ One conflict to resolve: banned words vs the messaging guide

The PMM messaging guide leans on words the content writing guidelines **ban**: "seamless," "optimize," "streamline," and "all-in-one" as a descriptor of 7shifts. The value props above are quoted as written, but when you turn them into published content, rewrite per [`voice.md`](voice.md):
- "all-in-one" → **"in one app" / "one easy-to-use app"** (the idea is sanctioned; the literal phrase as a descriptor is not)
- "seamlessly connect" → **"connect" / "bring together"**
- "optimize / streamline" → **"make easier" / "simplify"**
