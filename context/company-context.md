## In one breath

7shifts is the team-management platform built exclusively for restaurants: scheduling, time clocking, team communication, payroll, tip management, and labor-cost management in one app. Founded 2014 by Jordan Boesch in Saskatoon, traced back to a tool he built for his dad's Quiznos franchises. The purpose: free up managers' time so they can focus on their staff and customers. Trusted by 50,000+ restaurant locations and 1M+ restaurant workers.

- **Tagline:** Great teams make great restaurants.
- **Guiding idea:** On your side, by your side.
- **The hero is always the operator and the staff. 7shifts is the trustworthy sidekick.**

## Business model

Subscription SaaS, priced per location by tier, plus add-ons. Product-Led Growth motion (free plan + feature limits steer users to paid), tightly integrated with a data-driven sales motion (product + firmographic + dormancy data surfaces high-quality users for proactive outreach). Dedicated Mid-Market/Franchise and Partnerships teams capture the rest.

| Tier | Price / location / mo | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Comp (Free) | $0 | Single location, up to 15 employees. Basic scheduling, one-way announcements. |
| Essentials | $44.99 | Up to 30 employees. Labor budgeting, templates, reporting, messaging, basic time clocking. |
| Pro | $89.99 | Unlimited employees. Compliance tools, onboarding (US), time-off accruals, performance, Manager Log Book. |
| Premium | $149.99 | Tip Management, Payroll, Task Management, advanced labor forecasting, permission templates. |

**Add-ons:** Payroll ($39.99/location + $6/employee), Task Management ($12.99/mo), Tip Management ($49.99 + $1/transaction for instant payouts).

**Strategic arc:** evolved from a single-point scheduling tool into a restaurant operating system by adding payroll, hiring, and tip management. More value, more upsell, higher switching costs.

## Products

- **Scheduling** (core): drag-and-drop builder, free mobile app, availability + shift swaps. Higher tiers auto-schedule from POS sales forecasts, weather, and labor budgets.
- **Time Tracking (7punches):** integrated time-clock app. Geofencing + photo verification to prevent time theft; break/overtime compliance.
- **Team Communication:** announcements, group + 1:1 chat, digital Manager Logbook. Replaces texts and consumer chat apps.
- **7shifts Payroll** (add-on): syncs scheduling, punches, and tips to kill manual data entry.
- **Tip Management:** automated tip-pool calculation and distribution from custom rules + POS sales data.
- **Task Management:** digital opening/closing/in-shift checklists with completion tracking.
- **Hiring & Onboarding:** job posts, applicant tracking, new-hire paperwork.
- **Employee Engagement:** pulse surveys, engagement scoring, feedback. Built to reduce turnover.

## Market position

Vertical SaaS: the specialized workforce platform for restaurants, vs generic horizontal tools restaurants have to bend to fit. TAM ~700,000 North American restaurants.

**Competitors:** restaurant-focused (HotSchedules/Fourth, Sling/Toast); horizontal and cheaper (Homebase, Deputy, When I Work); and the real threat, POS platforms bundling "good enough" scheduling (Toast, Square, Lightspeed).

**Why 7shifts wins:**
1. **Restaurant-only focus.** In 2016-17 Boesch fired 70% of non-restaurant customers to commit to the vertical. That focus buys deep domain features (POS-integrated labor forecasting, complex tip pooling) horizontals can't match.
2. **Deep integration ecosystem.** Two-way integrations with 65+ POS systems, plus payroll/accounting/HR, and an open API. 7shifts is the labor layer on top of the restaurant's POS "brain."
3. **PLG.** Free Comp plan removes the barrier to entry and builds familiarity.
4. **Founder authenticity.** The Quiznos origin story resonates with operators.

## Challenges and opportunities

**Challenges / threats:**
- **Payroll execution.** The biggest strategic bet has had real failures in public reviews: missed tax filings, wrong calculations, penalties for the restaurants using it. A major trust killer if unresolved (and a sensitivity to handle carefully in any external content).
- **POS bundling.** Toast/Square/etc. own the payment relationship and can give away "good enough" scheduling, commoditizing the core product.
- **Price pressure** from horizontals (Homebase, Deputy).
- **Industry margin compression** limits operators' tech budgets.

**Opportunities:**
- **Payroll + platform stickiness.** If trust is earned, integrated payroll creates high switching costs and lifts revenue per customer, moving 7shifts from nice-to-have to must-have OS.
- **Underserved market.** ~65% of new customers still use pen, paper, or Excel. Huge non-technological base to capture.
- **Data moat.** 50,000+ restaurants of data to train forecasting/ML.

## Strategic direction

- **Double down on Tip Management and Payroll** — the pivot from scheduling tool to end-to-end "restaurant OS."
- **Own the full employee lifecycle:** hire, train, schedule, pay, retain.
- **Capital efficiency.** The small $6M Series C extension (late 2024, after the $80M 2022 Series C) signals profitability over growth-at-all-costs.
- **Move upmarket.** Next-generation multi-entity payroll (late 2025) targets higher-value multi-unit groups.
- **Long-term vision:** a thriving, connected restaurant ecosystem powered by 7shifts' data and domain knowledge.
